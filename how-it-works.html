How the Marketplace works?

1
DOWNLOAD THE BARTERUNIT APP AND REGISTER

Create an account or Login in seconds.

2
POST YOUR GOODS, SERVICES AND UNWANTED ITEMS

You may barter your goods, services and unwanted items in exchange for other goods, services and unwanted and/or Barter Units.

3
SEARCH OUR MARKETPLACE

View and search all postings using our filters. Use the ‘I’m Searching For’ (ISF) feature to help us better match your desired goods and services.

4
MAKE
AN OFFER

Find something you want? Make an offer using our unique Barter Board. Its designed to make bartering easy fun and efficient.

5
COMMUNICATE VIA OUR MESSAGE BOARD

Use our messages board to chat, negotiate and make arrangements with other members in real-time.

6
EXCHANGE
BARTER UNITS

Is your trade offer NOT of equal value? Simply, use Barter Units to cover any possible value differences. Include Barter Units in your offer(s) for optimal responses and results.

POCKETBOOK AND PAYMENT SYSTEM

Your ‘Pocketbook’ is your wallet. Use it to manage your Barter Units. You can securely send, receive and redeem Barter Unit in real-time. For faster, safer payment transactions use our QR code feature to send and receive Barter Units as well.
